Make the Next Developer Podcast!

A podcast is a way to share the skills that you have developed over your career with others.
 
It's also a way to connect with other developers and learn new things to improve your skill set.

Keep your show from "Podfading"...

As of 2018 Apple has added 660,000 podcasts to its podcast directory with close to 28 million episodes published.

That may seem like a lot of competition, but the average show makes less than 50 episodes in it's lifetime. While some podcasts last for years, a large number make it less than 15 episodes. 

Most podcasts don't meet their creators expectations, not because of lack of listeners, but lack of planning.
